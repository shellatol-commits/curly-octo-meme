Totem Man

Totem Man is a humorous Minecraft resource pack that replaces the standard Totem of Undying texture with a stylized human “Totem Man” character. It keeps the functionality of the original item while changing its visual appearance for comedic or aesthetic purposes.

🔧 Installation Guide
Step 1: Download

Visit the Modrinth page:
https://modrinth.com/resourcepack/totem-man

Click Download for your Minecraft version.

Step 2: Install

Open Minecraft (Java Edition)

Go to Options → Resource Packs

Click Open Pack Folder

Drag the downloaded .zip file into the folder

Enable the pack in-game

No extraction required.

🧩 Compatibility

✅ Minecraft: Java Edition

❌ Not compatible with Bedrock Edition

⚠️ May conflict with other packs that modify the Totem of Undying texture

If another pack edits the same item, whichever pack is higher in the resource order will take priority.

🎨 Design Concept

Totem Man transforms the mystical golden idol into a more human-like figure. This design leans into Minecraft’s community tradition of humorous texture edits, similar to meme-style resource packs.

Purpose:

Add personality

Increase humor during survival gameplay

Make clutch saves more entertaining

📜 Trivia

The Totem of Undying normally drops from Evokers in Woodland Mansions and Raids.

Totem Man keeps the original use animation and activation glow.

Popular among players who enjoy meme-based texture packs.
